Tampa Bay Testing


Tampa Bay Testing is dedicated to work with you to get yourself back in order. Michele Durrance, PsyD, a licensed clinical psychologist in Florida, Arizona, Nevada and California (PSY17402), and her team provide their patients with a comprehensive array of therapy services with a personalized approach. Dr. Durrance specializes in psychological assessments for those who struggle with ADHD, family onset depression, and psychoeducational assessments. In addition to assessments, she also provides cognitive behavioral therapy for adults, adolescents and children.
