About Michele

Dr. Durrance-Miller is a Licensed Psychologist in the state of Florida who specializes in psycho- educational and psychological assessment. She has been in practice in the Tampa Bay area since 2003 and has experience in the public, private and charter school settings, as well as hospitals, courtrooms, and private practice.
Dr. Durrance-Miller has dedicated her practice to providing comprehensive evaluations to meet the unique needs of each client. She understands that the most important part of the evaluation is not the diagnosis obtained but how the results will lead to effective school-based and treatment recommendations. The goal of psychological testing is to identify individual strengths and weaknesses which will optimize one’s potential.
On a personal note, Dr. Durrance-Miller has three children, one of which has been diagnosed with ADHD/Autism, making her uniquely qualified to provide supports to families of children with special needs.
